Shuklaphanta Agro Alleviative Nepal

Shuklaphanta Agro Alleviative Nepal(SHAAN) is a hatchery for certain local breeds of chicken located at Bheemdatta 15, kanchanpur district of Nepal.Various breeds like Kadaknath,New Hampshire, Black Australorp, Naked Neck (Ghati Khuile), Frizzle chicken (pwakh ulte) and Shakhini are currently available here.
